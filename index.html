Common questions

How long is a session, and how often do we meet?

Most sessions are 50 minutes, once a week. We keep a steady rhythm, and adjust only when it truly supports your care.

How do online sessions work?

Secure video, simple setup. You just need a private space, stable internet, and a way to speak comfortably.

Do you take insurance, and what are the fees?

This is a portfolio design demo, so fees and insurance details are not listed. In a real practice, you would see clear pricing, insurance options, and payment details in this section.

How long does therapy take?

It depends on your goals. Some people choose a focused 8 to 12 session block, others prefer longer support. We decide together, and we can adjust as you grow.
